Monstera Peru

Monstera Peru widely known to be native to subtropical, jungle environments. this plant originally came from the jungles of Thailand as well as tropical areas within america


As the Monstera Peru naturally climbs throughout its area, this plant requires vertical support such of a moss pole to assist and ensure it is growing at an optimal speed. With proper preparation, this plant is capable of exceeding 1 meter. Once the Monstera Peru has exceeded lengths preferable, it is reccomended to properly prune the plant


Monstera Peru is unique as its leaves do not have fenestration. the leafs of Monstera Peru are thick and of a scaly texture.










How to care for your new Monstera Peru


Light
This plant should also be placed in a area with pletiful indirect sunlight. It's optimal temperature is to be in between 65 to 80 F.

Water
Ensure that your Mostera Peru is watered once per week. Humidity levels are to be at 50% minimum

Soil
Monsteras typically perform well in a potting mix that retains moisture but also drains well. They prefer slightly more acidic pH soil, from 5.5-6.5. A soil mix containing 1 part peat moss/coco coir, 1 part perlite, and 4 parts pine bark fines is an excellent mix for Monsteras
