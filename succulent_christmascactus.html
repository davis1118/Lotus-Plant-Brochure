Christmas Cactus

This plant is native to southeastern Brazil and gets its name from reference to the time of year it blooms.


A Christmas cacti is a sustainable plant, capable of enduring heat as well as droughts rather well. This allows the plant to survive areas that hotter than usual .


Christmas cactus is unique as its leaves do not have fenestration. The leafs of cactus are thick and of a scaly texture.








How to care for your new Christmas Cactus

Light
This plant should be placed in a area with pletiful indirect sunlight. The optimal temperature for the plant to be in is between 70 to 80 F.

Water
Ensure that your Christmas Cactus is watered once a week. Humidity levels are to be around 50%.

Soil
Christmas Cactus, like other succulents, prefer well aerated and well draining soil. Using a succulent soil mix with lots of drainage, such as perlite, is ideal for this plant.
